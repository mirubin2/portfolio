About

Marketing that moves people.

I'm an experienced leader in B2C and B2B marketing, partnerships, events, and brand activations — known for initiating innovative, culturally relevant concepts that build brand awareness, drive consumer engagement, and increase sales across the entertainment, sports, and retail ecosystems.

At National CineMedia, I led the modernization and monetization of cinema advertising through first-to-market content and creator partnerships, generating $2M+ in incremental revenue and hundreds of millions of impressions. As the U.S. Representative to Cannes Lions, I grew Young Lions participation 20% year-over-year and opened $1M+ in new business pipelines.

At Macy's, I revamped the national multicultural event and marketing strategy, owning total campaign strategy across omni-channel media, creative, budget, and experiential elements from conception through execution — including Macy's largest Pride presence in company history at World Pride 2019.

I started my career in sports marketing at the LA Dodgers, where I negotiated all on-field celebrity talent appearances, relaunched the iconic celebrity softball game, created the first VIP experiences at Fan Fest, and launched the organization's first loyalty program for 20,000+ season ticket holders.
